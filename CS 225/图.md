## 一些图的术语

$$
\begin{aligned}
& G=(V,E) \\
& \lvert V \rvert = n \\
& \lvert E \rvert =m
\end{aligned}
$$

![[assets/Pasted image 20240426142828.png | 200]]

- Incident edges:  
    $\mathrm{I}(v)= \{ (x, v) \in E \}$
-  $\mathrm{Degree}(v)=\lvert \mathrm{I} \rvert$
- Adjacent Vertices:  
    $\mathrm{A}(v) = \{x:(x, v) \in E\}$
- Path: 被边连起来的点的序列
- Cycle: 起点和重点相同的Path
- Simple Path: 没有自环和多重边（两个点之间有多条边）
- Subgraph:  
  $G'=(V',E')$:  
  $V' \in V$, $E' \in E$, and  
  $(u, v) \in E' \implies u \in V',\quad v \in V'$
