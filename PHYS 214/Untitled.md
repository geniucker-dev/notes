## Harmonic Waveform (in 1-D)

$$y(t) = A \cos(\frac{2\pi}{\lambda} (x-vt)) \equiv A \cos(kx - 2\pi f t) \equiv A \cos(k(x-vt))$$

- $k \equiv \frac{2\pi}{\lambda}$: wave number 波数
- $\omega \equiv 2\pi f$: angular frequency

常用关系：

- $v = \frac{\lambda}{T} = \lambda f$
- $v = \frac{\omega}{k}$
- $v = \frac{\lambda}{T}$
- $\omega = 2\pi f$
