- ***Boyle’s Law***: $PV = k$  
- ***Charles’s Law***: $V=bT$  
- ***Avogadro’s Law***: $V=an$  
=>***The Ideal Gas Law***: $PV=nRT$  
$R = 0.08206 L\cdot atm/(mol\cdot K)$  
$R = 8.314J/(mol\dot K)$  

### Kinetic Energy
$KE_{avg}=\frac32RT$ ($KE_{avg}$ is per mole)  
$u_{rms}=\sqrt{\frac{3RT}{M}}$  

### Real Gas
***van der Waals Equation***:  
$[P_{obs}+a(n/V)^2]\times (V-nb)=nRT$
