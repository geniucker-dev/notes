
permutation matrix (置换矩阵): exchange two rows or columns of a matrix
e.g.  
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1 \\
0 & 1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b & c \\
d & e & f \\
g & h & i
\end{bmatrix}=
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b & c \\
g & h & i \\
d & e & f
\end{bmatrix}
$$
here
$$
P = 
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1 \\
0 & 1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}
$$
another example
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b & c \\
d & e & f \\
g & h & i
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1 \\
0 & 1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}=
\begin{bmatrix}
a & c & b \\
d & f & e \\
g & i & h
\end{bmatrix}
$$
here
$$
P =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1 \\
0 & 1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}
$$



# Calculate the inverse of a matrix
Gauss-Jordan
If I want to calculate the inverse of  
$$
A =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 \\
3 & 4
\end{bmatrix}
$$
I can do this
$$
\left[
\begin{array}{cc|cc}
1 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\
3 & 4 & 0 & 1
\end{array}
\right]
$$
$$
\left[
\begin{array}{cc|cc}
1 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & -2 & -3 & 1
\end{array}
\right]
$$
$$
\left[
\begin{array}{cc|cc}
1 & 0 & -2 & 1 \\
0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2} & -\frac{1}{2}
\end{array}
\right]
$$
so
$$
A^{-1} =
\begin{bmatrix}
-2 & 1 \\
\frac{3}{2} & -\frac{1}{2}
\end{bmatrix}
$$
