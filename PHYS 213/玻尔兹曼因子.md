## 玻尔兹曼问题的策略

1. 写出microstates和他们的能量
2. 概率$P(A) = \frac{e^{-\frac{E_{A}}{kT}}}{\sum e^{-\frac{E_{A}}{kT}}}$
3. 平均能量$U(T) = \sum_{i} \mathrm{P}(\text{state i,T})$
4. 热容$\mathrm{C}_{V}(t) = \frac{\mathrm{d}U}{\mathrm{d}T}$
5. 熵$S(T) = \int_{0}^{T} \frac{C_{V}(T)}{T} \, \mathrm{d}T$
